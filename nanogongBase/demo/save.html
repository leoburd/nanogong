Adding New Buttons to Original NanoGong

The applet right now has:

  • Playback of "woof.wav"
  • Record a new (maximum 20 minute) voice recording
  • Stop or pause in the middle of a recording or playback
  • Speed up or slow down the playback
  • Save the most recent recording
  • When the mouse is placed over the timer on the bottom, it displays the total amount of time and the the amount of time elapsed in the recording.

What I want to accomplish:

  • Save/Upload to a different server (not just your hard disk)
  • Run multiple applets on the same site

More information about NanoGong can be obtained from http://gong.ust.hk/nanogong/.
